    About Us

Welcome to our Hotel

Raddegoda Walawwa is located in Ridigama and features a garden. With a terrace, the property also features a restaurant. Free WiFi is included throughout the property. At the hotel, each room is fitted with a desk. Rooms have a private bathroom. Guest rooms have a seating area. A continental or Asian breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. Raddegoda Walawwa offers an outdoor pool. When guests need guidance on where to visit, the reception will be happy to provide advice. Kandy is 30 km from the accommodation, while Dambulla is 40 km away. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International Airport, 80 km from Raddegoda Walawwa
